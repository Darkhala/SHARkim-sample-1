Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse the Shop, open a product you like, and reach us via WhatsApp or Email. You can also add items to the cart and checkout via WhatsApp or Email.

How long does delivery take?

Most orders are delivered within 1–3 business days depending on your location. We'll confirm the delivery timeline after your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept MPESA and cash on delivery in supported areas. For large orders, a deposit might be requested.

Do products have warranties?

Many products include manufacturer warranties. Warranty terms vary by product and brand—please check the product page or ask us.
